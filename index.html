About Leptospirosis

Leptospirosis is an illness caused by the bacterium Leptospira, which can be present in contaminated water or soil. It can impact various animals and humans. If left untreated, leptospirosis can result in severe health issues in people, including kidney damage, meningitis (inflammation of the protective membranes around the brain and spinal cord), liver failure, breathing difficulties, and potentially death.
